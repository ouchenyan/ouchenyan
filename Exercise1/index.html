How to Stay Warm and Stylish During Winter

Layering Your Clothes

  1. Start with a thin layer underneath.
  2. Put on thin, tight clothes.
  3. Add a vest or sweater.
  4. Get a good winter coat.

Making Your Look Stylish

  1. Wear a hat.
  2. Keep your hands warm.
  3. Cover up the obvious holes in your wardrobe.
  4. Get the right shoes.
